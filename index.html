About Me

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Verona, Italy, specializing in advanced anomaly detection algorithms for industrial manufacturing environments. Working under the supervision of Francesco Setti, my research focuses on developing robust deep learning architectures for real-time defect detection and quality control in industrial automation.

My interdisciplinary background spans computer vision, natural language processing, and industrial AI. I received my Master's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (2019-2021) under Xiaohong Liu, focusing on sentiment analysis and NLP. I hold a Bachelor's degree from PMAS Arid Agriculture University Rawalpindi (2013-2017).

With 9+ peer-reviewed publications in top-tier venues including ICCV, ECCV, and ICIAP, my current focus is on advancing industrial AI through scalable and interpretable anomaly detection systems that transform manufacturing quality assurance and enable predictive maintenance strategies.
