Muhammad Aqeel

Postdoctoral Research Fellow working on unsupervised anomaly detection for industrial manufacturing.

Department of Computer Science · University of Verona, Italy

Email·Scholar·GitHub·LinkedIn

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Verona, Italy, developing anomaly detection methods for industrial manufacturing systems. I completed my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Verona under the supervision of Francesco Setti, specializing in unsupervised and meta-learning approaches for anomaly detection.

I earned my Master's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications under Xiaohong Liu, focusing on natural language processing and sentiment analysis, and my Bachelor's degree from PMAS Arid Agriculture University, Pakistan. My research bridges theoretical AI and practical industrial applications, with a focus on efficient, scalable anomaly detection for real-world manufacturing environments.
